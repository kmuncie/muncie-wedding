We love you!!

We deeply love all of you and cherish the many ways you have had deep and lasting impact on our lives. In the new system we will have a giant gathering on top of a mountain meadow where we will enjoy great food and spend hours talking and sharing stories of our lives and how Jehovah has blessed us.

For now however, we must be modest and live within our means. The Watchtower has some wonderful articles on how to increase the joy and dignity of a wedding, and we have studied these numerous times in our family worship. For that reason we have attempted to keep our wedding very small and modest. We have no bridal party, we are trying to invite only around 50-75, and the reception will be a small simple affair.

Instead of inviting hundreds of you to the expense of travel, lodging, and our inevitable desire to provide an unforgettable event, we would much rather have a small dignified wedding which will bring honor to Jehovah and the marriage arrangement... and then have many smaller gatherings in the coming months where we can enjoy your company and love in a meaningful way.

We are saying many prayers that you will understand our reasons for these decisions and know how much we truly do love each of you and look forward to enjoying the rest of our lives with you doing many wonderful things together. Thank you for your support and for caring about us both so much!
